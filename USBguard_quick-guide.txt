1. Show current devices:

	sudo usbguard list-devices
	
2. Plug your USB

3. Execute step1.

4. Search for you device on the list, normally at the end, note the ID number at the start of the line.

5. Enable your device:

	sudo usbguard allow-device <ID_number>
	
6. Input/Output the desired information from the device.

7. Disable the device again:

	sudo usbguard reject-device <ID_number>
	
8. Close terminal
